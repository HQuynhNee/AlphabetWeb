Introduction

In today's fast-growing technology era, integrating modern teaching methods into children's education is essential. "Learning Alphabets" is an interactive desktop application designed to help children explore the Vietnamese alphabet in a fun and engaging way.

Developed using the Waterfall model, the project ensures a structured approach from analysis to testing. The app features a colorful interface where children interact with mystery eggs that reveal letters and illustrated words, making learning more enjoyable.

By blending education with technology, "Learning Alphabets" enhances cognitive skills, curiosity, and engagement, offering a fun and effective way to master the alphabet!
